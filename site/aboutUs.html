ABOUT US

You read that correctly! Mann Co. has taken its FIRST BOLD STRIDE into internet commerce, planting its size-16 boot confidently up the plump, mysterious backside of the FUTURE. And why? Because I just learned that the Internet is a THING THAT EXISTS.

It seems that one of the hidden directives of my staff has been to keep the Internet's existence from me, under the assumption that if I found out about this miraculous invention I would immediately BANKRUPT THE COMPANY trying to buy a country or have somebody killed.

It wasn't until Gorilla Wrestling Fridays, my attempt at employee morale building, that someone let the cat out of the bag. A gorilla'd gotten one of my accountants in a headlock, and I won't lie to you, even with the knife he was losing pretty badly. It looked grim, reader.

"Email my mother that I love her," he croaked, his pupils contracting. "Damn it, son, of course I will," I said, because as everyone here at Mann Co. has seen time and again, there is nothing--NOTHING--I won't do for an employee dying in my arms.

"Quickly!" I said, jumping up. "Somebody get me email!"

That's when it was explained to me what the Internet was. Three things have happened since:

    1.) I am now the proud owner of England
    2.) the perverts at Anagram YourName.com should be ASHAMED OF THEMSELVES, and I have seen to it that they won't be bothering anybody in the future
    3.) There is now a "virtual" Mann Co. store available. Browse, buy, design, sell and wear Mann Co.'s ever-growing catalog of fine products with your BARE HANDS all without leaving the COMFORT OF YOUR CHAIRS!

I bet you'd love to know all the intricate details of how the Mann Co. Store works. Well, that makes one of us, because I don't. In fact, thinking about it just now made my brain invent something vastly more interesting in defense. And while you're doing that, remind yourself periodically that I'm about to spend roughly the same period of time parachute-boxing.
